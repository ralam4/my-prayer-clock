Frequently Asked Questions

Is My Prayer Clock really free?

Yes — completely free with no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no ads. It's built as a service to the Muslim community.

How accurate are the prayer times?

Prayer times are calculated using your precise GPS location with established Islamic calculation methods including ISNA, MWL, Egyptian, and Umm al-Qura. Times update automatically as you travel.

Does the app track my data or require an account?

No. My Prayer Clock collects zero personal data. No account needed, no analytics, no third-party tracking. Your location stays on your device.

What features does My Prayer Clock include?

All 5 daily prayer times, Qibla compass, Ramadan fasting tracker with suhoor and iftar times, prayer streak tracking, home screen widgets, and both light and dark modes. Works on iPhone with iOS 26 or later.
